## Data Collection

### Social Page

::: tip IMPORTANT NOTICE
We collect IP addresses via our Analytics API EndPoints to monitor site usage and performance.
:::

As stated on our [Social Page](https://notreal003.github.io/social), we do not collect personal data from users on this section of our website, except for IP addresses as noted above. These are gathered through tools like Cloudflare Insights to help us understand site traffic trends. Your privacy is paramount to us, and no other identifying information is stored or processed from this page.

### Request Page

**Notice: We collect user data to provide, secure, and improve our services.**

When you log in with your Discord account or sign up with an email address on our [Request Page](https://request.notreal003.org), we collect the following data:

- **Account ID** (for users signed in with Discord or Google): To link your account and enable login functionality.
- **Email address**: To verify your identity and send service-related updates.
- **Username and avatar**: To personalize your experience on the site.
- **IP address**: For security purposes (e.g., detecting suspicious activity).
- **Device and browser information (user agent)**: To optimize compatibility and troubleshoot issues.

All collected data is retained **until the service is permanently discontinued** and becomes unavailable. This ensures historical, operational, and security continuity.

To request access or deletion of your data, contact [support@notreal003.org](mailto:support@notreal003.org).
However, **response timelines (7, 30, or 90 days) are not guaranteed**, and your request **may be denied or rejected** depending on operational capacity, legal, or technical reasons.

### Player Stats Check

When you search for a player on our [Player Stats Check site](https://notreal003.github.io/player), we collect:

- **Player name**: Only for successfully searched players, stored to improve search functionality and track usage trends.

No other personal data is collected from this feature.

## Data Sharing Policy

We maintain a strict policy regarding your privacy.

**We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.**

Specifically:
- **No Advertisers:** We do not share your information with marketing agencies, advertisers, or ad networks.
- **No Third-Party Sales:** We do not sell your data to any external organizations.

### Service Providers
We utilize trusted infrastructure providers solely to host and secure our services. While data passes through these systems to allow the website to function, these providers are not authorized to use your data for their own marketing purposes:
- **Cloudflare & Railway:** Used for hosting, security, and performance.
- **Google Analytics:** Used strictly for internal traffic analysis.

## Communication Preferences

We may use your **email address** exclusively to send you:
- Essential service updates and announcements (e.g., security alerts, feature changes).
- Internal newsletters regarding *our* specific projects.

**We will not send you third-party advertisements or promotional offers from external companies.**

You can **opt out** of non-essential communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or by contacting us at [support@notreal003.org](mailto:support@notreal003.org).
Opt-out requests will be processed when feasible, but response or removal timeframes are **not guaranteed**.
