Become a partner and earn revenue by promoting Sentralia. As a creator, you will receive a unique **Creator Code**. When your community uses this code at checkout, they get a discount, and you earn a percentage of the sale.

::: info How it works
1. **You promote Sentralia** using your unique code.
2. **Your viewers get a discount** (e.g., 5-10% off) when purchasing the repository.
3. **You get paid** a revenue share directly to your Tebex Wallet.
:::

## Prerequisites

Before applying, ensure you meet the following requirements:
* You have an active community (YouTube, Discord, etc.).
* You are capable of receiving payouts via PayPal or Bank Transfer.
* You are willing to register for a **Tebex Wallet** account.

## Step 1: Create a Tebex Wallet

To receive payouts, you must register a secure wallet with our payment processor, Tebex.

1. Go to [https://wallet.tebex.io/register](https://wallet.tebex.io/register).
2. Create an account and verify your email.
3. Complete the **Identity Verification** steps (required for payouts).
4. Link your preferred **Payout Method** (PayPal or Bank Account).

::: warning Important
We cannot manually send money to you. You **must** have a verified Tebex Wallet to receive automated payouts from the Creator Code system.
:::

## Step 2: Get Your Wallet Reference

Once your wallet is set up, we need your unique **Wallet Reference** to link your code to your account.

1. Log in to your [Tebex Wallet](https://wallet.tebex.io/).
2. Navigate to **Withdraw > Your Details**.
3. Look for the **Wallet Reference** (it usually looks like `w-12345-67890`).
4. Copy this reference ID.

## Step 3: Submit Your Application

To request your code, you must submit a formal ticket through our support portal.

**Submit your Application at:** [https://request.notreal003.org/support](https://request.notreal003.org/support)

::: danger IMPORTANT: Provide Information At Once
To ensure a fast approval process, please provide **all** the following details in your initial request description. Incomplete requests will be closed automatically.

**Copy and paste this template into your request:**

* **Desired Creator Code:** (e.g., `YOURNAME`)
* **Tebex Wallet Reference:** (e.g., `w-12345-67890`)
* **Community Link:** (Link to your YouTube channel or Discord server)
:::

Once submitted, our team will review your channel and, if approved, link your code to your specific Wallet Reference. You will receive a notification via the support portal when your code is active.

## FAQ

**When do I get paid?**
Funds earned via Creator Codes are usually settled after **30 days** to account for refunds or chargebacks. You can withdraw them from your Tebex Wallet immediately after settlement.

**Can I change my code later?**
Codes are permanent once created, but exceptions can be made for rebranding. Please contact support.
